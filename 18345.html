            The Kingsbury Hotel Features

          We were kindly offered a better room without
          additional payment. The king suite is huge and
          has all the necessary equipment, e.g. a kettle,

          There are several beaches at hand. You can

          several supermarkets around, the closest one is

          a short way off, as well. The personnel and owner
          are very friendly and helpful. We got useful tips and
          even an excursion. They also organized a boat safari

          and curry were really delicious and at a very reasonable
          price.
